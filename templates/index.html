Where many languages combine to form one

MELEX Lexicon

MELEX LEXICON is essentially Malaysian Language. The everyday conversation language that Malaysian uses to communicate with one another

Malaysia is well knwon for it citizen that consist of many ethnicity. Such factor have contributed alot to the evolutuon of languages in our everyday conversation. These languages evolves from multiple ethnicity that had already span multiple language. Malaysian language is the combination of formal English and Bahsa Melayu. Not only that it had alsoinfused with a lot of our informal word that had become part of our everyday vocabulary. These words are meaningful to usually only us Malaysian.

POS Tag

Each sentence have words that have part of speech(POS) that provides meaning to the entire sentence

In the world of IT, for computer to understand text, we need them to first understand our language. One of the way is through POS. There are multiple source out there that provides such POS tagging however there are usually for English or their national languages only. By far there aren't any resources out in the market that provides such function for our Malaysian Language. The point of this system is to allow user to inout a Malaysian Language, the system are still able to tag it properly so that machine can learn and understand whta we human are trying to convey with our Malaysian Language.
